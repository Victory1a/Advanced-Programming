Our Organic Promise

Healthy pizza is only possible with ingredients you can trust.

Mission

GreenSlice exists to prove pizza can be both delicious and nutritious. We use organic ingredients for every layer: crust, sauce, cheese, and toppings—always.

How we started

We began as a small kitchen focused on clean eating. When we realized most “healthy pizza” still used processed ingredients, we built a menu using fully organic components from trusted suppliers.

What “organic & healthy” means here

Crust

Organic flour, long fermentation, and optional whole-grain crusts.

Sauce

Organic tomatoes, herbs, olive oil—no artificial flavors or added sugar.

Cheese

Organic dairy and dairy-free cashew options, both made without additives.
